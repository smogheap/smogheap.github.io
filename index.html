about

smogheap was established in 2013 as a disorganized organization with the simple goal of creating games, just for fun.

"smog", a dirtier form of "vapor" found out in the open. Many projects started here may be vaporware for years (or forever), and everything is waving about in full public view right here.

"heap", a not-terribly-tidy-sounding collection and a nod to our scrapheap, an experiment in sharing art, resources, tools, inspiration, and dead-ends.
